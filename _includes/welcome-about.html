Al and Esperanza Cabrera always dreamed of owning their own restaurant. In 1999, they opened their Hartford location, and in ___ opened a second restaurant in Mansfield. Now both in their 80s and semi-retired, Al and Esperanza leave the daily running of the restaurants to family. Son William operates the Mansfield location, while sons (and twins) Jose and Pablo, along with their sister-in-law Silvia, run Coyote Flaco in Hartford.
